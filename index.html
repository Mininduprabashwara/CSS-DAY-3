Marvel is one of the most famous entertainment brands in the world, loved by millions of fans. It started as a comic book company in 1939 and later grew into a massive movie franchise called the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Marvel is home to iconic superheroes like Iron Man, Spider-Man, Captain America, Thor, and the Hulk. These heroes often team up as "The Avengers" to protect the Earth from dangerous villains like Thanos. What makes Marvel special is that its characters have relatable human flaws, making their stories emotional and inspiring. Today, Marvel continues to capture our imagination through exciting movies, TV shows, and video games.
